The three phases

Each phase builds on the last. The workouts don't change shape — the numbers do.

Phase 1
Foundation
Weeks 1–4
◎
Abs
3×12 hanging leg raises, ab wheel, planks. Learn the moves with full control.
◎
Biceps
3×12 wide-grip curls, incline curls, hammer curls. Establish the width pattern.
◎
Legs
4×6 squat, 3×10 RDL. Moderate weight, full depth, perfect form.
◎
Stamina
3×40s intervals on rower or bike. 10 min mobility after every leg day.
Phase 2
Build
Weeks 5–8
◉
Abs
Add weight to leg raises. Ab wheel goes to 4×10. Weighted cable crunch enters.
◉
Biceps
Volume up to 4 sets. Heavier load, slower eccentric (3 sec down on every curl).
◉
Legs
Squat moves to heavy sets + back-off sets. RDL weight increases each week.
◉
Stamina
Intervals extend to 5×1min on/off. Mobility holds increase to 90 sec.
Phase 3
Peak
Weeks 9–12
●
Abs
Dragon flags. Weighted leg raises. 5 sets ab wheel. Core is visibly different.
●
Biceps
Peak contraction holds. 4×8 heavy incline, 3×10 EZ bar, drop sets wk 12.
●
Legs
Squat PR week in wk 11. Bulgarian split squat enters.
●
Stamina
20-min steady state + 5×1min sprint finishers. Splits deepen noticeably.
How to use this plan
🏋️
Log every set
Enter your actual lbs and reps for each set. The plan uses your logs to auto-calculate next-session targets and PRs.
📈
Progression rule
Hit the top of the rep range on every set, two sessions in a row, and the "Suggested" target bumps the weight — using the smallest jump you've actually made on that lift before.
⏱
Rest times matter
Written into every workout. Shorter rests build stamina. Longer rests build strength.
🗓
Missed a session?
Pick up where you left off. Two missed weeks in a row? Repeat that week before moving on.
Every session, written out

Log your weight (lbs) and reps for each set. Check off each set when done. PRs and targets update automatically.
